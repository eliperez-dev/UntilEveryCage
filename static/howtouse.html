How to Use This Map

This map exposes the global infrastructure of animal exploitation. Use it to investigate, document, and dismantle.

Getting Started

The overlay panel in the top-left corner of the map is your primary tool:

  • Search: Facility names, company names, or animal types (e.g., "Tyson", "cattle", "chickens")
  • Location: Select country, state, or province
  • Download CSV: Export the currently visible facilities as a CSV file for analysis and research
  • Reset: Clear all filters

Investigation Pathways

Public Company Research

For publicly traded companies (Tyson, JBS, Cargill), research SEC 10-K forms for board members, financials, legal issues, and operations.

SEC EDGAR Database

Corporate Connections

Map parent companies, subsidiaries, and business relationships.

APHIS Annual Reports

Copy the certificate number from the facility popup, paste it into the APHIS search tool, then click "view annual reports" Look carefully for exception reports and narratives in the e-column data.

APHIS Annual Reports Tool

University & Government Research

For academic institutions, research animal care committees and protocols. Use FOIA requests for government research facilities.

APHIS Inspection Database (US Only)

For US-based facilities only: Search by certificate number (in popup info), business name, or location. Find license status, inspection reports, violations, and contact info.

APHIS Inspection Database
